Meet Your New Garden Companion

Introducing your personal RipeYard robot – the gardener that never sleeps, never complains about the weather, and transforms your yard into a productive paradise without you lifting a finger. Have a long cold season? No problem. Your robot can build a beautiful greenhouse and grow gorgeous produce all year long.


The Complete Automated Cycle

Your robot handles the entire growing process from start to finish. It analyzes your soil, climate, and preferences to select ideal crops. Seeds are ordered, received, and planted with scientific accuracy. Your garden receives 24/7 attention through advanced sensors and AI, ensuring perfect timing for peak flavor and nutrition at harvest.


Self-Sustaining System

Your RipeYard robot comes with its own solar charging station, making it an environmentally friendly addition to your property that powers itself through the seasons. The system is designed to be completely self-sufficient, requiring no input from you beyond initial preferences.


Farm-to-Table Redefined

Connect with local chefs who can access your garden's real-time inventory. Receive custom meal suggestions based on what's currently ripe. Sell excess produce for passive income or share with neighbors to strengthen community bonds. Your robot manages all logistics and communications.


White Glove Onboarding

We personally introduce you to your robot with a comprehensive setup session. We'll help customize preferences and garden goals, set sharing, selling, and consumption priorities, establish your taste profile for optimal crop selection, and answer any questions as you begin your effortless gardening journey.


Smart Management Dashboard

Monitor your garden's progress, upcoming harvests, and earnings through our intuitive app – as involved or hands-off as you choose to be. Check in on your garden remotely, adjust preferences, or simply wait for the notifications that your dinner ingredients are ready to be harvested.
